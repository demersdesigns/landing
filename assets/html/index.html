Oh, hey there. I'm Paul Demers,

A Boston-based Purveyor of Fine Digital Experiences

For the last 15 years, I've been honing my design and development skills while working on projects for enterprise and startup companies alike. I strive to turn complex client ideas into scalable digital products and user flows. Over the years, I have been fortunate to help clients like Fox, Pearson, and Brightcove refine and improve their digital products and services. Currently I'm a senior experience designer with the stellar team at Cantina in Boston.

I take pride in being an active member of the Boston design and development community. As a teacher at Startup Institute, I have been able to help prepare students for jobs in the technology startup community. I have also volunteered with Resilient Coders, an organization that teaches young people from traditionally underserved communities how to code.

When not behind the computer, you can find me cycling around New England, noodling around on my drumset, or spending time with my three-legged lab shepherd, Booker.
